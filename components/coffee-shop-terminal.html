Coffee Shop Online

Terminal based Python project for the Cloud Support Engineer training program where working in groups we developed in one day a fully functional app for managing the orders of a coffee shop. The app allows customer to buy food, drinks and books and when the order is finished gives you the option of deliver it to your home. When the order is finished returns a bill.

  • Main Tools used: Python, Pandas, Numpy
  • Category: App Development
  • Date: January 2024
