Lottery Game

Try your luck running in a Jupyter Notebook this lottery game so you can know what to expect in advance while playing lottery. In this game you can simulate that you play lottery, let's say once every day during 100 years, and know how many times you will would have won the lottery. I won the lottery 2 times in 1000 years, would you be luckier than me?.
